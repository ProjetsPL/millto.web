A manhole is one of the most basic elements used in modern sewer systems. Sometimes it is called an inspection chamber or a access chaamber. As the name indicates, an inspection chambeer is used for inspection - it's a hole that allows for the sewer system control. An easy access to sewer pipes enables their easier cleaning, and in case of a malfunction one can react swiftly and eliminate the problem without the need to turn the whole system off. ## Manhole types available on the market Manholes are distinguished mostly by the type of material they are made of.
